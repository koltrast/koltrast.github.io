Who am I?

Hi, I'm Aurélien Mt., I'm a multidisciplinary artist based in France. Mostly used to work with cinematographic materials, I'm fascinated with their time relations and phenomenology.

Through my work, I reflects on time and its modality through cinematographic and new media arts. My research explores the existing permeability between different elements of cinema such as sound, image - fixed or moving - light, the passage from one image to the next, etc...
Computers has always been my main tools, recently I'm plunged myself into code learning to automate process and explore the world of generative art.
