Glámur
2016

Abandoned in 1966 and reputed to be haunted, the herring factory in Hjalteyri, located in the north of Iceland, is today partially transformed into an art center. Initially composed for this center and to be heard in this same place, Glámur is inscribed in this abandonment, in these deserted places, in the history of this small fishing village of about 46 inhabitants.
